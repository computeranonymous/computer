the plan
--------

- we eat food
- we go to a good pub
- we say 'computer'
- we drink

there isn't going to be organized talks, workshops, or otherwise. it's just a chance to meet good people and talk about good and bad things. we will talk computer a lot, but we'd also like to not talk computer too. 

we might have a talk, we might try and learn a thing, but at the end of the day we're just here not to be alone when we find ourselves saying "computer".

the rules
---------

- a standard code of conduct applies 
- if you enjoy hacker news, the works of ayn rand, or think feminism 'has gone too far' please note we may be hostile and/or throw rocks. 
- this is not an enterprenuerial meetup, nor is it networking. it is a support group.
- we're a very informal and ad-hoc group right now, so if you piss us off you won't be invited again. 
- the rules will become more explicit if we grow.
