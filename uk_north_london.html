The Meetings

The organisers are looking to create a non-drinking relaxed North London event as an alternative to the typical "meet in a pub, don't drink if you don't want to" event. We recommend people bring craft projects, instruments, or anything else people might enjoy.

This is an experiment.

Presently looking for meditation spaces to host a North London meetup. An ideal venue will have:

  • Lots of Space
  • Near two stations
  • Geek friendly
  • Comfortable seating
  • Serves Tea
  • Wheelchair friendly
