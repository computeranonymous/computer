The oddly named West London group are going to meet at: The Mad Hatter, 3-7 Stamford Street, London, SE1 9NY, on Thursday 24th October 2013, from about 18:30.

Future meet ups will actually be in the west, as opposed to central London, with the next potentially in Richmond, but this meet up is more to get an idea of where people might like to meet in future and to meet people initially. We can argue about whether Balham is an appealing place to meet, or perhaps Neasden? (Unfortunately meeting in the Neasden bunker probably isn't an option.)

All are welcome at this meet up!

Inspired by the North London group, if you want to bring craft projects or anything else people might enjoy, please do.

@squirmelia learnt how to do cross-stitch a few months ago, and would be happy to share skills and materials with anyone who would like to learn how to make cross-stitch space invaders, QR codes, etc!
