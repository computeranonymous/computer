We're going to be quite flexible with the Computer meetup in Oxford and not stick to "every nth Monday at location."

If you have any suggestions for venues, or if you want to organise the next Computer Oxford, get in touch with @omgmog or @lurst or @janepipistrelle via Twitter, or submit a GitHub issue to @omgmog's fork of computer.

Most of us idle in #oxford-geeks on irc.freenode.net if you want to chat, anybody is welcome.
