The Meetings

We haven't planned our first Meeting for Manchester yet, we're still working out where we want to hold it! If you have any ideas, fork the repository or send suggestions to me on twitter, @sjmarshy, whichever is easier.

Potential Venues

The Font Bar is super cheap, however it's small and noisy and gets pretty full, so it might not be ideal for a large group

Footage is larger than Font, but it can still be a little loud

The Bay Horse is more central than the others, located in the northern quater. Nice atmosphere, too.

Brew Dog always looked pretty interesting, it's meant to be good for beers if you're into that sort of thing, and it's nice and central.

We need a few more suggestions for venues really! If you have any in mind, Let me know!
